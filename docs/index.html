Powerful Features for Mental Wellness

🎯

Daily Mood Check-ins

Track your emotions with 6 intuitive mood categories. Visualize trends and patterns over time.

📝

Personal Journal

Capture thoughts and reflections with guided prompts. All entries are timestamped and searchable.

🧘

Mindful Activities

Engage with puzzles, breathing exercises, and guided journaling to boost your mental wellness.

Mood Analytics

Beautiful charts and statistics help you understand your emotional patterns and progress.

Responsive design that works perfectly on all devices with therapeutic colors and smooth animations.
